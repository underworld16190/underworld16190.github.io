1) Google mailbox archiving → Microsoft 365 migration

Problem

A customer needed historical Google email content archived into Microsoft 365 for continuity and retention. Actions

  • Coordinated prerequisites and access (handled securely; credentials not documented publicly).   • Resumed failed batches and documented progress for completion handoff. [3](https://suresystems.sharepoint.com/sites/ServiceTeam2/Shared%20Documents/Apps/Microsoft%20Teams%20Mailhook/Rapid%20Response%20(Dispatching)/EmailMessages_4_2026/New%20Unassigned%20Ticket%20on%20Escelation%20Board%20Please_2002778223.eml?web=1)
